
# Chef Pre-Seed Pitch Deck

A 12-15 slide investor presentation for Chef - the all-in-one hospitality platform that manages procurement, operations, P&L forecasting, and payments for restaurants.

---

## Brand & Design

- **Primary Color**: #F6F1E7 (warm cream background)
- **Accent Color**: #7A1E2D (burgundy/wine red for CTAs and highlights)
- **Typography**: Inter font family
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 optimized for projectors
- **Theme**: Light, elegant, high-contrast for projector visibility
- **Icons**: Lucide React library
- **Animations**: Moderate - animated charts, content reveals on slide navigation, smooth transitions

---

## Slide Structure (13 slides)

### 1. Title Slide
- Chef logo/name with tagline: "The Manual for Your Restaurant"
- Founder names (JL - CEO/COO, PJ - CTO)
- Subtle animated entrance

### 2. Problem Slide
- Hospitality is a margin game
- Restaurants juggle 8+ disconnected tools (POS, inventory, reservations, payments, procurement)
- No unified view of P&L prediction based on reservations and historical data

### 3. Solution Overview
- One-stop-platform covering ALL facets of restaurant operations
- AI-powered menu reading, ingredient estimation, cost prediction
- Animated feature reveal showing platform capabilities

### 4. Vision: Big Restaurant Data (BRD)
- The ultimate goal: become the data intermediary between chefs, restaurants, and suppliers
- Insights for suppliers: popular dishes, average covers, chef trends
- Partnership potential with Michelin, Gault Millau
- Animated roadmap visualization

### 5. Product Features
- Predictive P&L based on reservations + historical attendance
- AI menu scanning for ingredient/cost estimation  
- Recipe database from Michelin chefs
- Direct procurement integrations with wholesalers
- Loyalty/payments (POS integration)

### 6. Competitive Landscape
- Feature comparison matrix (Toast, Allo, Lightspeed, SIDES, etc.)
- Visual checkmarks showing Chef's comprehensive coverage
- Highlight: competitors cover 3-4 areas, Chef covers ALL

### 7. Business Model
- Tiered subscriptions: Appetizer → Starter → Main Course → Full Course
- Small fee per procurement order volume
- Percentage on payment volumes and loyalty discounts
- Animated pricing tier reveal

### 8. ICP & Go-to-Market
- **Tier 1**: Bistro groups, professional cafeterias (Lunchgarden), university cafeterias
- **Tier 2**: Standalone bistros (primary target for recipe database)
- **Tier 3**: Fast food chains, ethnic restaurants

### 9. Market Size (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- Belgium: ~20K restaurants (NACE codes)
- SAM: 60% (~12K restaurants)
- SOM: ~5% (1,000 restaurants)
- Target ARR: ~€4.8M (€399/month per restaurant)
- Animated bar chart showing market opportunity

### 10. Roadmap & Future Features
- Timetable integration for flex workers (gig-driven model)
- Gamification: monthly competition between restaurants for free subscriptions
- Influence on suppliers/growers based on ingredient demand predictions
- Review analysis integration (Feedelity-style)

### 11. Team
- JL - CEO/COO
- PJ - CTO
- Future CPO position: Hospitality Master + Business background

### 12. Ask & Use of Funds
- Pre-seed funding amount (placeholder for your input)
- Key milestones: MVP, first 50 restaurants, feature expansion

### 13. Closing Slide
- Contact information
- Tagline reinforcement
- Call to action
